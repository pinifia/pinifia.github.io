I will announce our new concert dates here. I have been singing actively in many bands since 2012. Right now, I am singing in the band Mental Damage. Since we are a new band, we have been practicing and have just played two concerts recently.

It would also be good to talk about my previous bands. For example, İki Yüz Yirmi was my second band between 2022-2023, and I still miss them. We had a lot of fun together. This picture, for example, is from Zorlu Performans Sanatları Merkezi. It was a fantastic stage with a great sound system, and we were the opening band for Yonca Evcimik at that school event, which is why we are standing in front of all those children.

With the same band, we went to Karaman by high-speed train and played at the university. It was a lot of fun because the audience was very engaged, and everyone in the school contributed together to create a "train" by holding each other. Guess who was the driver? =p One of my top memories in life. They also gave us many gifts, including this plaque, for which I am still very thankful.

While performing, a band called Cold Turkey noticed me, and I continued singing in their band for 2 years. It was one of the best band experiences because all the musicians were very skilled at what they do.

We also continued giving concerts locally, about 2-3 times a month.

Right now, I am continuing with Mental Damage. We basically play hard rock, and next month we will be on stage again. Follow us for more information on: Instagram.
