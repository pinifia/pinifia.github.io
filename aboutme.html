About me..

My first encounter with music started in elementary school when an Italian friend introduced me to Finale, a music composition software, approximately in 2002. I spent countless hours creating compositions in Finale, and at the same time, I began exploring different instruments at home, such as guitar and keyboard, experimenting and trying to create my own sounds.

In 2010, while attending Boğaziçi University, I began performing in jazz and rock ensembles. In 2012, I started taking formal vocal lessons, and since then, I have never stopped working on my voice. Over the years, I joined various music groups, performing extensively in genres such as rock, pop, funk, and R&B, accumulating over a decade of stage experience.

During my university years, I was also preparing for conservatory exams. After completing my Chemistry degree at Boğaziçi, I was accepted into the Music Department at Bilgi University, where I improved my skills in areas such as music theory, orchestration, arranging, recording, and sound production. While studying music, I performed in musicals and rock shows organized by ITU’s musical and rock clubs, gaining further stage experience and exposure to collaborative performance.

From my second year onward, I began working as a freelance composer, creating original music for various projects. Additionally, I participated in game jams as a musician, which allowed me to expand my skills in game music composition, sound design, and sound effects, greatly broadening my creative and technical horizons.

After graduating at the top of my class, I joined Zonex Entertainment Studios as a composer, where I focused on creating music for games. Later, I spent two years teaching music at an international school. Currently, I teach chemistry at another international school while continuing to compose, produce, and perform music actively.

Music has been a constant companion throughout my life, shaping both my personal and professional journey. Whether performing live, composing for games, or teaching, I strive to bring creativity, emotion, and dedication to every project I undertake.
